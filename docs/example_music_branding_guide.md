# Example Music Branding Guide (1980s Touring Identity)

---

## 🎨 Overview
Example Music is a fictional 1980s European touring company with a strong Nordic influence, combining logistics, broadcasting, and live music touring aesthetics.

Theme:
- 1980s retro-futurism (TRON-inspired)
- Scandinavian twilight lighting
- Industrial touring realism

---

---

## 🔵 Monogram (OB)

- Text: **OB**
- Meaning:
  - Odense Boldklub (visual inspiration)
  - Outside Broadcast (touring relevance)
- Shape: Circular badge
- Variants:
  - Flat white
  - Navy on white
  - Cyan glow (hero use only)

---

## 🔤 Typography

### 1. ExampleMusicDisplay (Primary)

Style:
- Geometric
- Wide spacing
- Tron-inspired

Usage:
- Logos
- Bus/truck branding
- Posters

Character rules:
- A = flat top
- O = rounded rectangle
- M = wide stance

---

### 2. ExampleMusicTech (Secondary)

Style:
- DIN-style
- Condensed
- Industrial

Usage:
- License plates (ETRK1, EBUS2)
- Equipment labels
- Logistics

## 🌍 Visual Identity Rules

- Always use navy + white base
- Red trim must be subtle
- Cyan glow only for hero/logo
- Maintain 1980s realism (no modern elements)

---

## 🚀 Usage

This branding applies to:
- Tour buses
- Logistics trucks
- Refreshment vans
- Backstage equipment
- Visual media
